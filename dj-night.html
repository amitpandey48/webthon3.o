About DJ Night & Dance Party

End your Nirvana 2.0 experience on the highest note with our spectacular DJ Night & Dance Party! This epic closing event brings together the best of electronic music, live performances, and high-energy entertainment to celebrate the culmination of our tech festival.

Featuring renowned DJs, live music performances, interactive entertainment, and an open dance floor under the stars, this night promises to be an unforgettable experience for all participants. Whether you're a dancing enthusiast or just want to unwind after the intensive tech sessions, this event offers something for everyone.

Event Guidelines

  • Free entry for all registered Nirvana 2.0 participants
  • Valid event wristband or ID required for entry
  • Comfortable dancing attire and shoes recommended
  • Stay hydrated - water stations available throughout the venue
  • Photography and videography allowed for personal use
  • Respect other participants and maintain appropriate behavior
  • Security and medical assistance available on-site
  • Event may be moved indoors in case of severe weather
  • No outside food or beverages allowed
  • Lost and found service available at information desk

Safety & Health Guidelines

  • Hand sanitizing stations available throughout the venue
  • First aid station with trained medical personnel on-site
  • Well-lit pathways and emergency exits clearly marked
  • Security personnel present to ensure everyone's safety
  • Noise levels monitored to protect hearing
  • Cool-down areas available for those who need a break
  • Emergency contact numbers displayed prominently
  • Weather updates and backup indoor venue prepared
