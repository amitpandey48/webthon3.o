Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers to common questions about Nirvana 2.0

How do I register for events?

You can register for events through our website by visiting the specific event pages and filling out the registration forms. Each event has its own registration process and requirements. You can also contact our registration team at register@nirvana2.0.com for assistance.

What are the event dates and venue?

Nirvana 2.0 will be held from March 15-17, 2025, at the Tech Campus in Innovation City. Different events will take place at various locations within the campus including auditoriums, labs, outdoor stages, and exhibition areas. Detailed venue information is available on each event's individual page.

Are there any registration fees?

Registration fees vary by event type. Many events are free for students, while some specialized workshops and competitions may have nominal fees. Detailed pricing information is available on each event's registration page. We also offer group discounts and scholarships for qualifying participants.

What should I bring to the events?

Requirements vary by event, but generally you should bring: valid student/professional ID, laptop (for technical workshops), comfortable clothing and shoes, water bottle, and any specific items mentioned in your event's requirements list. Detailed "what to bring" lists are provided for each event.

Is accommodation provided?

We provide accommodation assistance and can recommend nearby hotels with special rates for Nirvana 2.0 participants. Local participants are encouraged to commute daily. For out-of-town participants, we have partnered with several hotels and hostels to offer discounted rates. Contact us for accommodation assistance.

Can I participate in multiple events?

Yes! You can register for multiple events as long as they don't conflict with each other time-wise. We encourage participants to explore different areas of technology and culture. Our event schedule is designed to minimize conflicts and maximize participation opportunities.

Are there accessibility accommodations?

Absolutely! We are committed to making Nirvana 2.0 accessible to everyone. Our venues are wheelchair accessible, and we provide sign language interpreters, assistive listening devices, and other accommodations as needed. Please mention your specific needs during registration or contact our accessibility coordinator.

How can I become a sponsor or partner?

We welcome sponsorship and partnership opportunities! Contact our partnerships team at partnerships@nirvana2.0.com to discuss various sponsorship packages, exhibition opportunities, and collaborative possibilities. We offer different levels of sponsorship to match your organization's goals and budget.
