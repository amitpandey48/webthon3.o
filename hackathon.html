About the Hackathon

Join the most exciting 48-hour coding marathon of the year! The Nirvana 2.0 Hackathon brings together the brightest minds to solve real-world problems through innovative technology solutions. Whether you're a seasoned developer or just starting your coding journey, this event offers the perfect platform to showcase your skills, learn from experts, and win amazing prizes.

This year's theme focuses on "Technology for Social Good" - creating solutions that can make a positive impact on society. Participants will work in teams to develop applications, websites, mobile apps, or any tech solution that addresses current social challenges.

Rules & Guidelines

  • Teams must consist of 2-5 members
  • All team members must be currently enrolled college students
  • Projects must be built from scratch during the 48-hour period
  • Use of external APIs and libraries is allowed
  • All code must be submitted via GitHub repository
  • Final presentations must be completed within 5 minutes
  • Teams must stay within the designated venue throughout the event
  • No pre-existing code or projects are allowed
  • Judges' decisions are final and binding
  • All participants must follow the code of conduct

Eligibility Criteria

  • Must be a current college or university student
  • Valid student ID required for registration
  • Age between 18-25 years
  • Basic programming knowledge required
  • Laptop and necessary software must be brought by participants
  • Teams can be formed before or during the event
  • Registration deadline: March 10, 2025
