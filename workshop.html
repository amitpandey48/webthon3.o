About Technical Workshops & Seminars

Our comprehensive workshop program is designed to provide hands-on learning experiences across multiple technology domains. Led by industry experts and experienced professionals, these sessions combine theoretical knowledge with practical implementation to enhance your technical skills.

Whether you're a beginner looking to start your tech journey or an experienced student wanting to advance your skills, our workshops offer something for everyone. Each session includes interactive demonstrations, coding exercises, and real-world project work.

Workshop Guidelines

  • Participants must bring their own laptops
  • All necessary software will be provided or links shared in advance
  • Basic programming knowledge recommended for intermediate/advanced tracks
  • Maximum 25 participants per workshop session
  • All materials and resources will be provided
  • Certificates of completion will be awarded
  • Light refreshments included during breaks
  • Workshop materials available for download after sessions
  • Q&A sessions with instructors after each workshop
  • Networking opportunities with industry professionals

Prerequisites & Requirements

  • Beginner Track: No prior experience required
  • Intermediate Track: Basic programming knowledge
  • Advanced Track: Strong programming background
  • Laptop with minimum 4GB RAM and recent OS
  • Stable internet connection for software downloads
  • Valid student ID for verification
  • Registration must be completed 3 days before event
